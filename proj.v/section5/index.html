Domain 5: Managing and Negotiating Health Care Delivery Systems

- Overview -

The essentials and competencies in the area of Domain 5: Managing and Negotiating Health Care Delivery Systems were met through the epidemiology project assigned in course NG 742 Population Health for Advanced Practice Nursing. The epidemiology project integrates epidemiologic, biostatic, and other evidence-based methods into population-based healthcare.
